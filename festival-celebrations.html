Festival Celebrations

Experience the joy, colors, and traditions of festivals through these celebrations.

Ganapathi Stages

Lighting diyas, fireworks, and sweets mark the vibrant festival of Diwali, spreading joy and prosperity.

Ganapathi Mandapam

The festival of colors, Holi, brings people together with playful colors, water balloons, and festive sweets.

Temple Decorations

Beautifully adorned temples with flowers and lights create a divine ambiance for religious festivals.

Moharam Festival

The Moharam festival is observed with prayers, processions, and community gatherings honoring tradition.

Hanuman Jayanthi

Devotees celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi by installing idols, performing rituals, and immersing them in water bodies.

Makar Sankranti

Makar Sankranti is celebrated with kite flying, sweets made of sesame and jaggery, and cultural festivities.
